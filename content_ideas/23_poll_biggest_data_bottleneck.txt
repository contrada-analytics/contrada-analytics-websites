LINKEDIN POST IDEA #23: Poll - What's Your Team's Biggest Data Bottleneck?

TARGET AUDIENCE: CPG operations, analytics, commercial teams

HOOK:
Your team has the data.
But something is blocking you from using it effectively.

What's your biggest bottleneck? (Poll)

KEY POINTS:
- Interactive poll format drives engagement
- Four common bottlenecks across the data-to-decision journey
- Each option resonates with different teams
- Comment section becomes valuable discussion forum
- Reveals industry pain points and common challenges
- Follow-up post analyzing poll results

STRUCTURE:
1. Open with the premise (you have data, but can't use it)
2. Present the 4 poll options
3. Briefly explain each option
4. Ask for votes
5. Encourage comments about specific pain points
6. Follow up in comments with solutions for each bottleneck
7. Promise follow-up post with results and recommendations

WHY POLLS WORK:

1. HIGH ENGAGEMENT
- Easy to participate (one click)
- People want to see results
- Drives comments and discussion

2. AUDIENCE INSIGHTS
- Learn what your audience struggles with
- Tailor future content to biggest pain points
- Identify common patterns

3. ALGORITHM BOOST
- Polls get prioritized by LinkedIn algorithm
- Multiple engagement points (vote + comment + results check-in)
- Extended visibility (people return to see results)

4. RELATIONSHIP BUILDING
- Creates dialogue in comments
- Demonstrates you understand their challenges
- Positions you as thought leader who asks good questions

5. CONTENT PIPELINE
- Follow-up post analyzing results
- Individual posts for each option (4 more content pieces)
- Case studies showing how others solved each bottleneck

ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY:

- Post poll in morning (when CPG professionals are checking LinkedIn)
- Engage with every comment in first 2 hours
- Share preliminary results at 24 hours
- Final results + detailed follow-up at 1 week
- Tag people who had insightful comments in follow-up post

TONE: Curious, diagnostic, empathetic (you understand their pain)
